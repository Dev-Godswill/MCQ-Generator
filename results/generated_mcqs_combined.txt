## MCQ 1
**Question:** What was unusual about the Shakespear tree's fruit?
A) They were shaped like apples.
B) They had a golden shimmer. 
C) They tasted like grapes.
D) They only grew during the winter.
**Correct Answer:** B

## MCQ 2
**Question:** What did the villagers believe the Shakespear tree could do?
A) Grant eternal life.
B) Give a glimpse into the future. 
C) Cure any illness.
D) Control the weather.
**Correct Answer:** B

## MCQ 3
**Question:** What was Emma's dream?
A) To become a famous singer.
B) To travel the world.
C) To become a writer. 
D) To become the village leader.
**Correct Answer:** C

## MCQ 4
**Question:** What inspired the villagers to share their own stories?
A) Emma's determination to fulfill her dream. 
B) The beautiful colors of the Shakespear tree's leaves.
C) A traveling storyteller visiting Eldermere.
D) The delicious taste of the Shakespear fruit.
**Correct Answer:** A

## MCQ 5
**Question:** What is the main theme of the story?
A) The importance of following traditions.
B) The power of fear and superstition.
C) The danger of believing in magic.
D) The importance of nurturing dreams. 
**Correct Answer:** D
