## MCQ 
**Question:** What is the primary distinction between extreme and moderate supernaturalism regarding the meaning of life?

A) Extreme supernaturalism argues for God as purposive, while moderate supernaturalism focuses on God as infinite.
B) Extreme supernaturalism posits spiritual conditions as necessary for any meaning in life, while moderate supernaturalism sees them as essential for ultimate meaning. 
C) Extreme supernaturalism emphasizes God-centered views, while moderate supernaturalism leans towards soul-centered perspectives.
D) Extreme supernaturalism relies on the promise of eternal life, while moderate supernaturalism does not.

**Correct Answer:** B) Extreme supernaturalism posits spiritual conditions as necessary for any meaning in life, while moderate supernaturalism sees them as essential for ultimate meaning.
